Jen McFadden is Associate Director of Entrepreneurial Programs and a Lecturer at the Yale School of Management, where she helps students launch new ventures and learn that failure doesn't kill you. She serves on the board of the Yale Landscape Lab and is a co-Founder of Skillcrush, an online education company. Before joining Yale, she conducted research on innovation in the news industry at the Tow-Knight Center. When she is not working, spending time looking at a wall, or hanging out with her family, you can find her writing, working on a few random side projects, or on Twitter, engaging in her unhealthy and time-consuming habit of pointing out Donald Trump's flaws.
